The Anatomy

In Summary

Five things worth saying out loud.

i.
The program worked.
+$267K in net variance, 458% growth, with the same SKU category and team. This is now a real category for us.
ii.
It's a customer-acquisition story.
90% of the growth came from 215 brand-new cigar buyers — accounts that already trusted us for other categories.
iii.
Concentration is a risk.
Top 10 accounts = 36% of the variance. Smokers Heaven alone is $26K. We need to deepen the long tail.
iv.
Krauszers chain leaned in.
22 Krauszers locations contributed $15K in variance — proof the program scales across a chain when execution is consistent.
v.
Circus Wines is a separate problem.
Half the chain went dark on cigars. Likely not category-related. Schedule a buyer conversation before we lose more locations.
vi.
Year Two opportunity.
If we convert another 200 of our non-cigar-buying accounts at the same average, we double again. The playbook exists — replicate it.
